Frequently asked questions

  • How can I activate the game? After you have purchased the game on our website, you will see a window with an activation code. By logging into Steam/Epic Games Store, you can go to the activation page and enter the activation code.
  • How to return the game? Unfortunately, there is no refund function in GameStore yet. If you want to forget about the game, just uninstall it.
  • How do I buy the game? You can buy the game using our website and secure payment methods.
  • What methods of payment are available? You can pay for the game using PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Visa/Mastercard, or Bitcoin.
  • How can I contact you? You can contact us by email: gamestore@gmail.com
